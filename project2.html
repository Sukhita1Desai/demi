About Forest

Forests are vital ecosystems that provide many benefits to humans and animals. They are home to many plant and animal species, and are essential for maintaining the Earth's climate. Forests are large areas of land covered with trees, shrubs, and grasses.They are the largest terrestrial ecosystems on Earth. Forests are home to many wild animals, including tigers, elephants, and lions.
